Haifa Sharab

Chairman of the Board

She was born in the year 1948 AD and is considered one of the first women to enter the world of business and commerce in the Gaza Strip, and she held many administrative positions, including: Founding member and board member of the Middle East Laboratories Company for the Pharmaceutical and Cosmetics Manufacturing Company LLC. Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Gaza Central Warehouse Company for Pharmaceuticals and Medical Supplies. She has participated in many conferences and workshops related to local and international trade and economy, and has close relations with many businessmen, merchants, and public and private figures at the national level. She is a member of the Business Women Forum, in addition to her membership in the Palestinian Chamber of Commerce.
